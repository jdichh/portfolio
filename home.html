_about

My name is Jason Paul S. Dichoso, born and raised in the Philippines, and I aspire to be a quality Front End Web Developer. I like to learn things about front end development, and apply those lessons into my projects.

I originally planned to be in the field of hardware since I'm pretty good with computers, and I did freelance jobs with that in mind. However, I got bored of it. I already had prior exposure to software development as I graduated from Letran-Calamba with an IT degree, but I decided to focus on learning front-end web development during the tail end of 2022.

I admit that I'm still not where I want to be with my current skills, but I am determined to be strong in this field.

As for my hobbies, I like to run, play the electric guitar, listen to music, fiddle around with computers, occasionally play RPGs and racing games, and that's about it. Some might say my life is boring, but I couldn't really care less about what they think.
