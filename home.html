about/

I'm Jason Paul Dichoso, and I aspire to be a quality front end developer. I love learning things about front end development, and apply what I learn into my projects.

I originally wanted to be in hardware jobs since I'm pretty good with those, and I did freelance jobs with that in mind. However, I got bored of it. Though I already had experience in software development, as I graduated from Letran-Calamba with an IT degree in 2023, I decided to specialize on front end development at the tail end of 2022.

I always remind myself that you can be better than you were yesterday, and always aim in learning new things.

As for my hobbies, I like to run, listen to music, play the electric guitar, occasionally play RPGs and racing games, and that's about it.
