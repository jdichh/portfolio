/about

My name is Jason Paul S. Dichoso, born and raised in the Philippines, and I aspire to be a quality Front End Web Developer. I like to learn things about front end development, and apply those lessons into my projects.

I originally planned to be in the field of hardware since I'm pretty good with computers, and I did freelance jobs with that in mind. However, I got bored of it. I already had prior exposure to software development as I graduated from Letran-Calamba with an IT degree, but I decided to focus on front-end web development during the tail end of 2022.

I admit that I'm still not where I want to be with my current skillset, but I am determined to reach the top of the mountain.

As for my hobbies, I play the electric guitar, listen to various genres of music, fiddle with computer hardware, and occasionally play RPGs and racing games. Some might say my life is boring, but I couldn't really care less about what they think.
